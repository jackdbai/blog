### Christendom

Books about Christians, Christianity, or Christian philosophy

- [The Autobiography of Gemma Galgani: The Book the Devil Tried to Burn](https://www.amazon.com/Autobiography-Gemma-Galgani-Devil-Tried/dp/172449158X)  
Biography, diary, and autobiography of St. Gemma Galgani, a mystic who lived in Italy during the turn of the 20th century. So called because her autobiography went missing and was only recovered after her confessor performed a long-distance exorcism. Upon its recovery, the book appeared to have scorch marks as if it had been through hellfire.
